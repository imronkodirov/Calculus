Curve Sketching Solutions

Problems: 1, 9, 13, 15, 21

---

Problem 1

\(y = x^3 + 3x^2\)

Domain

\((-\infty,\infty)\)

First Derivative

\(y' = 3x^2 + 6x = 3x(x+2)\)

Critical Points

\(3x(x+2)=0\)
\(x=0, -2\)

\(y(0)=0\)
\(y(-2)=4\)

Critical points: (0,0), (-2,4)

Second Derivative

\(y'' = 6x + 6\)

Inflection Point

\(6x+6=0\)
\(x=-1\)
\(y(-1)=2\)

Inflection: (-1,2)

Asymptotes

None

Problem 9

\(y = \frac{x}{x-1}\)

Domain

\(x \ne 1\)

Derivative

\(y' = \frac{-1}{(x-1)^2}\)

Critical Points

None

Second Derivative

\(y'' = \frac{2}{(x-1)^3}\)

Asymptotes

Vertical: \(x=1\)
Horizontal: \(y=1\)

---

Problem 13

\(y = \frac{x}{x^2-4}\)

Domain

\(x \ne -2,2\)

Derivative

\(y'=\frac{-x^2-4}{(x^2-4)^2}\)

Critical Points

None

Second Derivative

\(y''=\frac{2x(x^2+12)}{(x^2-4)^3}\)

Inflection

(0,0)

Asymptotes

Vertical: \(x=-2, x=2\)
Horizontal: \(y=0\)

---

Problem 15

\(y = \frac{x^2}{x^2+3}\)

Domain

\((-\infty,\infty)\)

Derivative

\(y'=\frac{6x}{(x^2+3)^2}\)

Critical Point

(0,0)

Second Derivative

\(y''=\frac{18(1-x^2)}{(x^2+3)^3}\)

Inflection Points

(-1, 1/4)
(1, 1/4)

Asymptote

\(y=1\)

---

Problem 21

\(y=(x-3)\sqrt{x}\)

Domain

\(x \ge 0\)

Derivative

\(y'=\frac{3(x-1)}{2\sqrt{x}}\)

Critical Point

(1,-2)

Second Derivative

\(y''=\frac{3(x+1)}{4x^{3/2}}\)

Intercepts

(0,0)
(3,0)
